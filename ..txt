Here’s a clean, ready-to-use **AI design/dev prompt** for your CRM:

---

**PROMPT:**

Build a modern **Sales CRM Web App (ArafatCRM)** for a business center that rents office spaces and workstations.

### 🎨 Design

* Primary color: **#2563EB (Royal Blue)**
* Style: clean, modern, minimal (SaaS dashboard style)
* Use soft shadows, rounded cards, and clear typography
* Responsive (desktop-first)

---

### 🧩 Core Features

#### 1. Sales Pipeline (Kanban Board)

Stages:

* New

* Qualified

* Meeting

* Proposal

* Negotiation

* Contract

* Drag & drop deals between stages

* Each card shows:

  * Client Name
  * Company Name
  * Expected Value (QAR)
  * Expected Close Date
  * Location (Barwa Alsadd / Element Westbay / Marina50 Lusail)
  * Space Type (Workstation / Office)

---

#### 2. Deals (Opportunities)

Fields:

* Client Name (linked)
* Payment Terms
* Currency (default QAR)
* Expected Value
* Expected Close Date
* Stage (pipeline)
* Location
* Space Type
* Broker (optional)

---

#### 3. Clients

Fields:

* Name
* Phone
* Email
* Company Name
* Source (dropdown):

  * Facebook
  * Instagram
  * TikTok
  * Broker
  * Google

---

#### 4. Brokers

Fields:

* Name
* Phone
* Company
* Contract Validity (from/to)

---

#### 5. Dashboard

Show:

* Total Deals
* Won Deals
* Lost Deals
* Revenue (QAR)
* Conversion Rate

---

#### 6. Reports

* Win vs Lost deals
* Filters:

  * Date range
  * Location
  * Broker

---

### ⚙️ Tech Stack

* Backend: **NestJS**
* Frontend: **React + Tailwind (TailAdmin Pro)**
* Use REST APIs
* Use clean modular architecture

---

### 📱 Pages

* Dashboard
* Pipeline (Kanban)
* Deals List
* Deal Details (Edit + Submit)
* Clients
* Brokers

---

### 🧠 Behavior

* Dragging a deal updates its stage
* “Won” and “Lost” are final statuses
* All values in **QAR**
* Clean forms with validation
* Fast and simple UX (no complexity)

---

### 🏢 Business Context

Arafat Business Center rents:

* Workstations
* Office Spaces

Locations:

* Barwa Alsadd
* Element Westbay
* Marina50 Lusail

---

Build it as a scalable, production-ready CRM with clean UI and strong UX focused on sales tracking and deal conversion.
